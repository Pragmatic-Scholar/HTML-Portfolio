Some of the best movies I've watched

List of movies off the top of my head

Inception

One of the best Nolan movie in my opinion.

Interstellar

Use of real world physics makes this one of the best science fiction movie for me.

Casino

Martin Scorsese and Robert de Niro is a cocktail never to be missed out on. Easily the best crime/mafia movie in my opinion.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

Wes Anderson at his best. If you aren't familiar with his movies, then you are in for a treat with this one.

Oldboy

Park Chan-wook and his dark humor will definitely take you through an emotional rollercoaster.

The Revenant

The best drama movie ever hand's down. Alejandro Gonzalez Innaritu's masterpiece for sure.

Prisoners

David Villenueve never disappoints. Neither does Hugh Jackman and Paul Dano in this one.

Seven

How can any list be complete without including atleast one of David Fincher's movie? If you've already watched it, check out his other movies.
